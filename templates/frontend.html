Efficace

A dashboard which analyses a twitter account to find if it is offensive and needs reporting.

Use cases include:

  • Terrorism
  • Online Bullying
  • Hate Speech

Censoring the most extreme content from the internet and doing a job that large corperations are failing to do

Input suspect Twitter account and click to see results!

This tool performs sentiment analysis on tweets and returns an option to report the account (this will show actual twitter account and tweets of username entered)
